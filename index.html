Hi! My name is Maria.

I’m a master’s student at the Polytechnic University of Catalonia.

My current focus is on my master’s thesis on using physics-informed neural networks for computer-assisted proofs in partial differential equations, with Javier Gómez Serrano (Brown University) and Tere M-Seara (UPC).
I graduated with a bachelor’s degrees in Mathematics and in Informatics Engineering in May 2023, sponsored by the CFIS program. In my last year, I was a visiting student at the NYU Center for Data Science, where I worked on my bachelor’s thesis with Joan Bruna on multiscale modeling of a closure problem using the scattering transform. During that time, I was affiliated with the M2LInES project.
During my undergraduate studies, I was a Maria Yzuel Fellow in Antonio Acín’s group at ICFO and I had summer internships at Google in 2020 and 2021.
My central interests lie in machine learning, especially when it intersects with climate science and partial differential equations.
